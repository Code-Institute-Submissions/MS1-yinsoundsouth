Ann Cecile (aka AnniShakti) first discovered Yoga in America as a child, going to classes alongside her mother. Throughout her own development, she studied with many great teachers from varying traditions and her Yoga practice and teachings are now driven by philosophy, alignment and discipline of asana, pranayama, meditation and Ayurveda.
Ann began teaching in 2004 at Anusara Yoga (an offshoot of Iyengar) and later taught Vinyasa and Bikram Yoga and Laughing Yoga. Her longtime passion for Kundalini yoga and Vipassana Meditation also greatly influence her unique approche, and in recent years, she has focused primarily on teaching Yin yoga -- a practice she believes extremely beneficial to our modern lives.
Ann also studied classical music throughout her youth; for her, music is one of the most sacred of human expressions through wich she has witnessed the power of spiritual chants and healing sounds paired with yoga practices. She is overjoyed to partner with Kes in this very unique training, where both her passions are shared and aligned.

Kes Cardoso found the path of Yoga as a teen, in Brazil. Later, when she was living in the UK during her university years, the path of Hatha and Kundalini Yoga became her tools of self-development and self-unraveling. Yoga and Sacred Sounds redifined her life and in 2015 she began training as a Yoga Teacher and Sound Healing Therapist, embarking in a new career and life journey.
Kes is experienced in Kundalini Yoga, Yin Yoga, Sound Healing, Yin-Yang Hatha Flow, Ashtanga, Aerial and Bikram Yoga. She has trained all over the world, building through the years a dynamic repertoire of traditions and practices. Her unique approach and teachings are rich and deeply grounded in ancient philosophy, conscious pranayama, vibrational asanas, kryia practices, chanting and sound meditation, quantum healing energy, and music performance and technique.
Today, Kes lives and teaches in the French Côte d'Azur. Her life's call is to evolve through Yoga and Sound for deep long lasting transformation, partnering with Ann to help you find the bridge between you and your upmost potential.

Our Immersive Program

Learn how to practice and teach Yin Yoga safely and consciously
while using Sound Healing as a therapeutical and meditative support

Our 50 Hour Yin Yoga & Sound Healing Training Immersion is a gentle, integrative and practical course that creates a sacred space where time expands and slows down, allowing you to find your deepest sense of self. This approach combines a unique blend of yin poses, sacred sounds, meditation through movement and stillness, experiential anatomy, principles of Chinese medicine, breath work, and mind-body ritual practices.

A life fully felt is a life fully lived

We know that the way we care for ourselves, others, and the environment is interrelated.  This is why it is so important for us that our training is held in nature — through open windy spaces, in valleys, mountain tops and rivers — so you can immerse in yourself and find your own loving guidance within. In this training you will dive deep in holistic well-being where the richness and subtleties of life are deeply experienced. As you follow your journey through the art of yoga and sound, we will help you cultivate the skills of practicing and guiding: you will learn to transmit from your own grace and power, while you develop your skills and potential as a yogi and as a teacher.

[IMAGE]

Who is it for

This training immersion is suitable for qualified yoga teachers wishing to improve their skills and teach Yin Yoga with Sacred Sounds, as well as for students and practioners of yoga, fitness or alternative healing modalities simply wishing to develop a deeper understanding of the Yin Yoga and Sound Healing approaches for their own personal or vocational purposes.

What you will learn

  • Knowledge and understanding of the Yin Yoga approach
  • Deep practice of the core Yin asanas, experiential learning and exploration of the ways these postures effect the deeper tissues of the body, release emotional tension and embody yin principles (difference between aesthetics and Functional Alignment)
  • Learn modifications and use of props
  • Deep Pranayama understanding (breathing techniques) for deeper relaxation and awakening in the energy body
  • The core concepts of the energy system (meridians) in Traditional Chinese Medicine and how it is influenced by the practice of Yin Yoga
  • Yin yoga sequences that work on specific meridian lines in the body
  • Knowledge of the Buddha’s core teachings on mindfulness meditation for integrating concentration and investigation methods into Yin Yoga classes or personal practice
  • Principles of sound healing and how to use tibethan bowls and supportive instruments for meditation and therapeutical purposes
  • Basics of therapeutical drumming and chanting
  • The integrative ritual approach between Yin Yoga and Sacred Sounds
  • Support for developing a home practice or daily sadhana

Our Location: Thousand Bridges

A Permacultural Retreat Center in Saint-Sauveur-sur-Tinée, France

[VIDEO]

Our property, a permacultural and retreat center, is a lovely family own farm situated inside the beauty and serenity of the southern alps, only one hour from Nice and the glittery coast of the French Riviera. Thousand Bridges is a sanctuary of peace, located in the midest of valeys and green fresh mountains, at the border of the Mercatour National Parc. This is a quiet place specially designed so you can be filled with prana and appreciate your connection with the wonders of nature.

At a Thousand Bridges you will

  • Enjoy the vitality of water: whether it’s wading in the river, watching the ducks in the pond, taking a dip in the natural pool or drinking the clear clean spring water
  • Savor nourishing, vegetarian meals prepared with organic and permacultured ingridients that are grown on site
  • Fill and clear your lungs with the pure air of the mountains, so you can get a boost of vitality, strengt and well being
  • On the final night, you will delight in a bonfire under the stars by the flowing river, engraving the memory of this sacred space and time forever in your heart
